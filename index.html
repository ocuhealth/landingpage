Faq

What is OcuHealth?
OcuHealth is a deep learning based application for monitoring eye health. This application helps detect eye health problems early by analyzing eye images taken from a smartphone camera.
Can OcuHealth be used to detect all types of eye diseases?
Currently, OcuHealth is designed to detect several common conditions that can be seen through eye images, such as cataracts, glaucoma, and some early signs of retinal disease.
Is my data safe when using OcuHealth?
We prioritize the privacy and security of user data. All photos and user data are processed locally on the device or encrypted when sent for analysis. We also comply with applicable data protection regulations.
How can I trust the results of this app?
Of course. Because the results obtained will be validated first by health professionals.
